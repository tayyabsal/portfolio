MAAMTA Program
Maternal Nutrition Initiative

The MAAMTA Program at Allied Hospital Faisalabad is a transformative maternal health initiative that registers and supports antenatal anemic mothers from early pregnancy all the way through post-delivery care.

Under this program, registered mothers receive free nutritional supplements and individually tailored diet plans at every stage — ensuring the safe delivery of healthy babies and the building of a stronger, well-nourished generation.
